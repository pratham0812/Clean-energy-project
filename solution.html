Quiz Results


1. What is the primary source of renewable energy?

Solar energy is a primary source of renewable energy, harnessed from the sun's rays.

2. Which of the following is a benefit of renewable energy?

Renewable energy sources reduce greenhouse gas emissions and help in decreasing the carbon footprint.

3. Which of these is considered a renewable energy source?

Wind energy, generated by wind turbines, is a clean and renewable energy source.

4. What is the main goal of sustainable energy?

Sustainable energy aims to minimize environmental damage and promote long-term energy solutions.

5. Which energy source is considered the cleanest?

Solar energy is considered one of the cleanest sources as it produces no emissions during operation.

6. What is the main challenge with renewable energy sources?

Renewable energy sources like solar and wind can be intermittent, depending on weather conditions.

7. What is one way to increase energy efficiency at home?

Using energy-efficient appliances reduces electricity consumption and saves costs.

8. Which country is the largest producer of solar energy?

China leads in solar energy production, investing heavily in solar power infrastructure.

9. What is the main advantage of wind power?

Wind power is a renewable energy source that can be harnessed indefinitely.

10. Which technology is used to convert sunlight directly into electricity?

Solar panels use photovoltaic cells to convert sunlight directly into electricity.

11. What does the term "energy efficiency" mean?

Energy efficiency means using less energy to provide the same level of energy service.

12. Which energy source is considered non-renewable?

Coal is a non-renewable energy source as it takes millions of years to form and is not replenished.

13. What is the main goal of the Paris Agreement?

The Paris Agreement aims to limit global warming by reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

14. What is a common method to harness geothermal energy?

Geothermal heat pumps use the earth's stable underground temperature to heat and cool buildings.

15. What is the primary reason for transitioning to renewable energy?

Transitioning to renewable energy reduces reliance on finite fossil fuels and promotes sustainability.
